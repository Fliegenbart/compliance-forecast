# Demo Story: Synthetic GMP Forecast Scenario

This demo story uses only synthetic sample data. It is designed for GMP consulting walkthroughs and does not represent a real client, real site, real product, or real quality-system record.

## Scenario Overview

The prototype shows a weekly advisory forecast for a fictional two-site quality system. The dashboard does not make GMP decisions. It highlights signals that should be reviewed by qualified human QA personnel.

## Story Beats

1. Packaging deviations are rising over the last six weeks. The pattern is intended to show how recurrence, backlog pressure, and workload can combine into a storm-style advisory signal.
2. CAPA-014 is overdue and linked to repeat Packaging deviations. This is a human-review prompt for CAPA adequacy and effectiveness-check design.
3. SOP-023 was recently revised, but related Packaging training remains incomplete. This demonstrates training drift after an SOP revision.
4. Sterile Filling has fewer deviations than Packaging, but the synthetic severity mix is higher. This supports a high-severity watch discussion.
5. QC Lab has recurring OOS/OOT-related investigations in QC Release Testing. This supports trend review for repeated investigation themes.
6. Engineering has validation-impacting change controls still open. This supports audit-readiness and validation governance discussion.
